# Wavelet Study 3 — Successive Halving Analysis

## Abstract

This study applies successive-halving search to **WaveletV3+Trend** stacks on M4-Yearly, exploring 14 wavelet families across basis dimensions, trend thetas dimensions, and active_g configurations. The search narrows 112 initial configurations to 29 across 3 rounds of increasing epoch budgets (7→15→30).

**Key Takeaways:**

- **Best configuration:** `Haar_bd4_lt_fcast_ttd3` (active_g=forecast) achieves mean OWA = **0.8061** with 5,070,095 parameters.
- **vs NBEATS-I+G (0.8057):** matches (Δ = +0.0004).
- **Parameter efficiency:** ~79% fewer parameters than NBEATS-G.
- **Total rows analysed:** 1194 (112 configs, 3 rounds).

- **CSV:** `/home/realdanielbyrne/GitHub/N-BEATS-Lightning/experiments/results/m4/wavelet_study_3_successive_results.csv`
